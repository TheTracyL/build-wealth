Fair Isaac Corporation. Landlords use it, and sometimes employers. FICO Scores are used by 90% of the top lenders.

FICO Score (Credit Score)

  • <580 = Poor
  • 580-669 = Fair
  • 670-739 = Good
  • 740-799 = Very Good
  • 800+ = Exceptional

What Affects Your Credit Score?

  • 35% - Payment History
  • 30% - Debt to Credit Ratio. They want to see less than 30%
  • 15% - Credit History Length
  • 10% - Credit Mix
  • 10% - New Accounts and Hard Inquiries
If your credit score is bad sign up for CreditKarma or NerdWallet and they will guide you to improving it.
